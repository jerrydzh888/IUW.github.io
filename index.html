Hosted by University at Buffalo Student Chapter of Human Factors & Ergonomics Society

Date: October 5th, 2024

Location: Hayes Hall, Buffalo, NY, USA

Description: The Inter-University Workshop (IUW) is an annual event that brings together researchers from the University of Waterloo, SUNY Buffalo, and the University of Toronto. It focuses on human factors and ergonomics research and is organized by students. Participants include graduate and undergraduate students who present research from both academic and industry settings. The workshop features presentations, keynote speeches, poster sessions, and networking opportunities, and is free and open to all. Registration is open to everyone, providing a platform for community members and industry professionals to learn about cutting-edge research in human factors.
